ABOUT


Valorant is a free-to-play multiplayer tactical first-person shooter developed and published by Riot Games. Launched in 2020, Valorant quickly gained popularity for its blend of precise gunplay, diverse character abilities, and strategic team-based gameplay.
Set in a near-future Earth, Valorant pits teams of attackers and defenders against each other in round-based matches. Players choose from a diverse cast of agents, each with unique abilities that can sway the tide of battle. Whether it's deploying smokescreens, summoning walls, or unleashing devastating ultimates, mastering these abilities is key to victory.

Combining the precise aiming mechanics of classic shooters with the diverse tactics of modern hero-based games, Valorant offers a high-stakes, skill-intensive experience. With its emphasis on teamwork, communication, and strategic thinking, every match presents new challenges and opportunities for players to outsmart their opponents.

Valorant has quickly become a prominent title in the esports scene, with professional tournaments drawing in millions of viewers worldwide. Its constantly evolving meta and regular updates ensure that players are always discovering new strategies and refining their skills.

Join millions of players around the globe and experience the adrenaline-pumping action of Valorant. Whether you're a seasoned veteran or a newcomer looking to prove your worth, Valorant offers an exhilarating gaming experience like no other.
